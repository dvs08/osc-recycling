Recycling and Sustainability

Enroll at Recycling and Sustainabilty


In order to be part of Recycling and Sustainability service group, the student must study at the Overseas School of Colombo. The student must be in 6th standard (MYP1) to 12th standard (DP2) to be eligible. Every semester there will be an online google form sent to all students for selecting service in which the student can enroll in this service from that google form. Students until 10th standard (MYP5) have an option do enroll in Recycling and Sustainability for 1 semester but it is compulsory for 11th and 12th standard students (DP 1 & 2) to be enrolled for both semesters. For more information for joining Recycling and Sustainability meet the service coordinator at the OSC campus.
