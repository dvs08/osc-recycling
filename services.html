Our Services

Service Recycling and Sustainabilty provides to the community.

Recycling

Every Thursday after school our service group collects recycling papers from each classroom, administration, and all school offices for the first half of our service session. During the trail, students engage and improve their collaborative skills.

Recyclable waste collection

R&S has been collecting paper and cardboard from the OSC campus and it also collects plastic water bottles during service and is now planning to begin collecting electronic waste because electronics can also be recycled. Through this R&S intends to further increase their impact on reducing the carbon footprint.

Canteen Managment

R&S provides containers in the canteen for assembling the waste food and then will be fed to the biogas. An external connection from the canteen to the biogas plant is also in progress by R&S through which students can directly empty their plate from the canteen straight to the biogas plant.

Biogas Plant

The biogas plant was installed on campus two years ago and R&S manages and regulates the biogas plant by feeding the waste food. The leaders also teach students and members about the mechanism of the biogas plant.

Recycling boxes

Recycling and Sustainability ensures every classroom and workplace on the campus has a recycling box to separate the waste. Every year in the 1st few lessons R&S designs and collects boxes which are then distributed as 'recycling boxes'.

Spreading Awareness

Recycling and Sustainability teaches every member the importance of sustainability for our planet, the impact, and how to manage our own waste efficiently along with the current situation in Sri Lanka with waste management. R&S also conducts events such as ‘Environment day’ to aware the entire school on waste management.

Service to the commnuity

Recycling and Sustainability provides service to the school campus by reducing and organizing the waste management. Every Thursday afternoon the service collects recyclable waste from all classrooms and workplaces on the school campus and sells it to the scrap dealer. The income earned goes toward sustainability projects such as providing more recyclable boxes, bags, etc. R&S helps the school community by recycling its major consumption which is paper along with plastic and will start on electronic waste. This service also spreads awareness about recycling and the impacts to the students by updating bulletin boards, environment days, etc. Recycling and Sustainability is one of the oldest services operating at the school campus and is playing a huge role in the school’s waste management.
