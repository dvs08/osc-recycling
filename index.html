What we do

The approaches we take on and out of the campus

Recycling

Every Thursday after school our service group collects recycling papers from each classroom, administration, and all school offices for the first half of our service session. During the trail, students engage and improve their collaborative skills.

Marketing

Recycling and Sustainability group deposits the recyclable papers to a scrap dealer and the revenues earned goes towards sustainable projects such as maintaining the biogas plant.

Managing Recycling Boxes

Recycling and Sustainability service group aims to provide every classroom a recycling box for waste separation.

Environmental Day

Every year during April, the Overseas School of Colombo hosts Environment day which is hosted by the Recycling and Sustainability group along with Reef Keepers.

A Little About Us

Here is a brief description about Recycling and Sustainability

Recycling and Sustainability service group is one of the oldest services operating at the Overseas School of Colombo. It provides an opportunity for students from 7th to 12th grade to take a step forward in improving the environment by helping the school and the community in reducing the amount of carbon released in the environment and regulating waste management. Recycling and Sustainability’s main aim is to reduce the carbon footprint on the environment along with spreading awareness about waste management and the environment to the school community. Through this service students also get a chance in learning about the global climate issues in Sri Lanka and also around the world and students also involve in creating projects which are approaches in recovering the global climate with emphasis on Sri Lanka’s waste management. Throughout the semester with Recycling and Sustainability, many organizations working towards waste management in Sri Lankan are invited by our service in which students get chances to share their ideas with the organizations, further learn about waste management and possible solutions, and can also get involved in larger projects with the organization .

Recycling and Sustainability teaches the approaches every person can take to make this planet a better place to live in school and out of school. The learning students gain from this service can be applied everywhere in their lives and can also be spread to others in the world which will help in reducing the climate footprint on the environment and improve the global climate.

We strongly encourage the three R's throughout the community and try to spread awareness as far as we can in order to make an impact.
